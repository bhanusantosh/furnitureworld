About us

For 6-decades, Furniture World has been the city’s premium furniture store, offering the largest collection of leather sofa sets, recliner sofa sets and more, sourced from across the world and also manufactured by our maven craftsmen.

The company’s flagship store at Banjara Hills has more than 1lakh sq ft display area, showcasing over 2000+ products. Through its retail chain, across Hyderabad and Vizag, Furniture World presents an exquisite range that includes neo-classical, grand-classical, royal to contemporary furniture and more.

It started its humble journey with 1 single retail outlet selling furniture and today has transformed into a group catering to a large segment of consumers in both retail & projects.
